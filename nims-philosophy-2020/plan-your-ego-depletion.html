Plan Your Ego Depletion

When you wake up to begin your day, you have a limited amount of mental resources to use throughout the day. And every decision you make, from choosing your outfit to rewording your emails drain ("depletes") this mental resource ("ego"). This is known as ego depletion. This mental energy is also used up every time you exercise your self-control and willpower.

We operate similar to a battery. But unlike a battery which performs consistently until it reaches 0%, our mental performance itself also depletes throughout the day. In other words, the earlier it is in the day, the better suited you are for tasks that require a lot of thinking and willpower.

So it's important to plan your day accordingly and ask yourself how you want your daily ego to deplete. Understand that you can only achieve so much in a day and that tasks that require more cognition and willpower should be addressed early.
