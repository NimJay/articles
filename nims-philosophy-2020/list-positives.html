List Positives

Our minds have something called a "negativity bias". This means that our minds are stimulated more by negative events than positive events.

For this reason, it's important to force ourselves to be more positive. A good way to do this is to create a habit of listing things you are grateful for. For instance, you could list things that you are thankful for, every morning when you wake up. It could be as simple as your ability to access the Internet of your ability to avoid hunger, but it could do wonders for your well-being.
