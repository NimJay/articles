Be Mindful

Our life — or at least our conscious experience — is really just one long train of thought. And we need to learn to steer the tracks.

Put another way, the number of thoughts you will have from this very instant until the day you die is finite. This finite set of thoughts basically paints the remainder of the experience you call "life". Hence, it's vital to regularly pause your thinking and pick and choose exactly what thoughts to have. In other words, it's important to be mindful.

Many topics of thought are simply useless, and will not bring any positive changes to your life or the life of others. For instance, thinking about an embarrassing moment from the past, or a family problem you aren't willing to solve will not bring anyone any good. But thinking about a positive memory or about the next steps of a personal project will.
